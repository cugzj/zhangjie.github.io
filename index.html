Short Bio

Jie ZHANG is currently a Post-doctoral Fellow in the Department of Computing at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (香港理工大学), Hong Kong SAR, China, where she received her Ph.D. degree in 2022, supervised by Prof. Song Guo, Fellow of IEEE. Before that, she received the B.E. and M.E. degree from China University of Geosciences (中国地质大学), Wuhan, China, supervised by Prof. Deze Zeng.

Her research interest broadly lies in the areas of edge computing, federated learning, distributed AI systems and foundation models. Some specific topics such as heterogeneous training, personalization for federated learning, and inference acceleration for pre-trained large models are highly involved.
